1. The Injection Moulding Bottleneck

Typically the main manufacturing method for the production of mobile phone casings is injection moulding. Adhesives Technologies offers insight into what can happen when injection moulding goes wrong. Their corrosive additives consumed tooling, produced vapour that burned moulds, and turned a 12-week lead time into a 24-week delay.

Incorporating Industry 4.0 technologies such as additive manufacturing and digital twins can help to stretch the possibilities rendered impossible by the rigidity of injection moulding. Industry 4.0 provides a shift from centralised to de-centralised manufacturing. Merit3D used photopolymer carbon DLS (digital light synthesis) to rapidly prototype iterations in a single day, proving that high-speed printing can replace traditional methods.
